Module Overview

The dissertation module represents the culmination of your undergraduate degree, providing an opportunity to demonstrate your skills, knowledge, and creativity through an independent project. This module provides structured guidance and support throughout your project journey, from initial development through to final implementation.

These sessions focus on the practical aspects of managing and executing your dissertation project. You will learn essential development practices, project management tools, and design methodologies that are crucial for professional software development.

Important: These sessions provide guidance and frameworks, but the success of your dissertation depends on your commitment to regular development work in your own time. Consistent effort throughout the year is essential.
